Permutation vs Combination

1. the basic permutation and Combination

1.1 formula

select k from n objects

  • k sequence (order matter & no repetition)
    $\frac{n!}{(n-k)!}$
  • k sequence (order matter & repetition)
    $n^{k}$
  • k subset (order does not matter & no repetition)
    $\tbinom{n}{k}$
    • k subset (order does not matter & repetition)
      $\tbinom{n+k-1}{n-1}$
      to better understand the formula, n is number of types and k is the actual number. eg:A machine produces a 9-digit code from the numbers {1,2,3,4,5,6}. So, ùëò=9 and ùëõ=6.
